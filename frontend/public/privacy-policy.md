# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## 1. Introduction

{{companyLegalName}} ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you apply for positions through our recruitment platform.

## 2. Information We Collect

When you apply for a position, we collect the following personal information:

- **Contact Information:** Full name, email address, and phone number (optional)
- **Application Data:** Position applied for, date of application, application source
- **CV/résumé:** Your curriculum vitae (CV/résumé) and any documents you submit as part of your application
- **Consent Records:** GDPR consent status and timestamp

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

- To process and evaluate your job application
- To communicate with you regarding your application status
- To conduct technical assessments if you advance in the recruitment process
- To comply with legal obligations and maintain recruitment records
- To improve our recruitment processes through anonymized statistical analysis

## 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do **NOT** sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your data with:

- **Internal Team:** Your application is reviewed by our recruitment team and hiring managers who have a legitimate need to access your information
- **Assessment Platforms:** If you advance in our recruitment process and are invited to complete technical assessments, we will share your email address with our third-party assessment platform providers to send you assessment invitations and track completion

All third-party service providers are contractually required to:

- Maintain the confidentiality and security of your information
- Use your data only for the purposes we specify
- Comply with applicable data protection laws

## 5. Data Retention

Your personal data will be retained for **{{dataRetentionMonths}} months** from the date of your application submission.

After this retention period expires, your data will be automatically **anonymized** (not deleted entirely). Anonymization means:

- Your CV/résumé and any attached documents will be permanently deleted"
- Your name will be replaced with "DELETED_USER"
- Your email will be replaced with a generic identifier (e.g., "deleted-12345@anonymized.local")
- Your phone number will be removed
- Your application record will be marked with an ANONYMIZED status

**Why anonymization instead of complete deletion?**

Anonymization allows us to preserve valuable recruitment statistics (such as "X applications received for Position Y" or "average time-to-hire") while completely removing your personally identifiable information. This approach balances your privacy rights with our legitimate business interests in maintaining accurate historical records.

After anonymization, it is **impossible** to identify you from the remaining data.

## 6. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are a resident of the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

### 6.1 Right to Access

You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. We will provide this data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format (JSON).

### 6.2 Right to Rectification

You have the right to request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold about you.

### 6.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You have the right to request deletion or anonymization of your personal data at any time before the automatic retention period expires. We will process your request within 48 hours of verification.

**Note:** Even after anonymization, we retain non-personally-identifiable aggregate data for statistical purposes.

### 6.4 Right to Restrict Processing

You have the right to request that we limit how we process your personal data under certain circumstances (e.g., while we verify the accuracy of your data).

### 6.5 Right to Data Portability

You have the right to receive your personal data in a portable format and transmit it to another data controller.

### 6.6 Right to Object

You have the right to object to processing of your personal data based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes.

### 6.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

Where we rely on your consent to process your personal data, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

For security reasons, deletion requests require email verification:

## 8. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

### Security Measures Include:

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS
- **Secure Storage:** Personal data is stored in secure cloud infrastructure with access controls and encryption at rest
- **Access Controls:** Access to personal data is restricted on a need-to-know basis and limited to authorized personnel only
- **Authentication:** Multi-factor authentication is required for administrative access
- **Regular Audits:** We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- **Logging and Monitoring:** All access to personal data is logged and monitored for suspicious activity
- **Secure Development:** Our code follows secure development practices and undergoes security reviews

Despite our security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Browser Storage and Tracking Technologies

Our recruitment platform uses **browser local storage** (localStorage) for the following essential purposes:

- **Authentication:** To store your authentication token and maintain secure access after login
- **Session Persistence:** To keep you logged in between browser sessions

**Important distinctions:**

- We use **localStorage**, not cookies. localStorage data is only accessible by JavaScript in your browser and is never automatically sent to our servers with requests.
- Your authentication data remains on your device until you explicitly log out or clear your browser data.

We **do NOT** use:

- Third-party tracking cookies
- Advertising or marketing cookies
- Analytics cookies that track individual users across websites
- Social media tracking pixels
- Browser fingerprinting techniques

You can clear your localStorage data through your browser settings (Developer Tools > Application > Local Storage). Clearing this data will log you out of the platform.
